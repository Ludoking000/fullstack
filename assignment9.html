Economic Growth Projections for 2024

The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has projected a growth rate of 6.5% for the Indian economy in 2024. This growth is attributed to increased consumer spending and a boost in investments.

India's G20 Presidency and Initiatives

India has taken on the presidency of the G20 for 2023, focusing on sustainable development and climate change. The country aims to foster international collaboration on various global challenges, including food security and health care.

New Education Policy Reforms

The Government of India is implementing reforms under the New Education Policy (NEP) aimed at improving the quality of education. This includes integrating technology into classrooms and enhancing vocational training opportunities.
